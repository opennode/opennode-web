Waldur

Open-source multi-cloud and HPC management platform

MIT License
  • Integrated marketplace for publishing and managing cloud services
  • Self-service portal with federated identity support (SAML, LDAP, national ID)
  • Built-in billing, accounting, and cost tracking
  • Advanced reporting — real-time, historical, and predictive analytics
  • Role-based access control with delegated permissions
  • Support ticketing with third-party helpdesk integration
  • Full audit trail and compliance logging
  • API-first design with comprehensive REST API

About OpenNode

OpenNode was founded in 2011 to deliver professional cloud architecture and implementation services. Over the years, we have carried out cloud infrastructure projects — from enterprise deployments to government-level solutions — across Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and Oceania.

Today, our primary focus is the development and support of Waldur — an open-source platform co-developed with the University of Tartu. Waldur is used by research and education networks, government clouds, and HPC centers across Europe.

Our team consists of experienced cloud architects, DevOps specialists, and full-stack developers, augmented by AI coding agents to accelerate development and maintain code quality.
